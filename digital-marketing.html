Course Syllabus

Detailed Digital Marketing Roadmap

This diploma program is structured in a step-by-step format so students can understand the field clearly and grow with confidence.

Module 1: Computer & Internet Basics

Understand the basics of computers, browsers, internet tools, email usage, and online working environments.

  • Basic computer usage
  • Internet fundamentals
  • Browser tools
  • Search engines
  • Professional Gmail usage

Module 2: Introduction to Digital Marketing

Learn what digital marketing is, how businesses use it, and why it is important in today’s online world.

  • What is Digital Marketing?
  • Traditional vs Digital Marketing
  • Marketing channels
  • Audience targeting basics
  • Business growth online

Module 3: Website & Landing Page Basics

Understand how websites and landing pages support digital campaigns and online lead generation.

  • What is a website?
  • Domain & hosting basics
  • Landing pages
  • Lead generation concepts
  • Call-to-action understanding

Module 4: Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Learn how websites rank on Google and how businesses get free organic traffic through SEO.

  • What is SEO?
  • Keyword research basics
  • On-page SEO
  • Off-page SEO
  • Local SEO basics

Module 5: Social Media Marketing (SMM)

Learn how businesses use social media platforms to build audience, engagement, and brand awareness.

  • Instagram marketing
  • Facebook marketing
  • LinkedIn basics
  • YouTube basics
  • Hashtag & engagement strategy

Module 6: Content Marketing

Understand how content is used to attract, educate, engage, and convert online audiences.

  • Content strategy basics
  • Social media content
  • Blog writing basics
  • Content calendar
  • Educational vs promotional content

Module 7: Graphic & Creative Marketing Basics

Learn the role of visual creativity in branding, social media, ad creatives, and digital campaigns.

  • Creative marketing basics
  • Branding concepts
  • Poster and social post planning
  • Visual consistency
  • Ad creative basics

Module 8: Paid Ads / Performance Marketing

Understand how businesses use paid ads to generate leads, traffic, and sales.

  • Introduction to Meta Ads
  • Introduction to Google Ads
  • Campaign basics
  • Audience targeting
  • Lead generation campaigns

Module 9: Email Marketing

Learn how email marketing helps businesses communicate, nurture leads, and retain customers.

  • Email marketing basics
  • Email list concepts
  • Promotional emails
  • Newsletter basics
  • Email writing basics

Module 10: Analytics & Tracking

Learn how marketers measure traffic, campaign performance, audience behavior, and conversions.

  • Analytics basics
  • Campaign tracking
  • Clicks, CTR, leads
  • Conversion understanding
  • Performance reporting basics

Module 11: Personal Branding & Freelancing

Understand how to use digital marketing skills for personal growth, freelancing, and client work.

  • Personal branding basics
  • LinkedIn profile growth
  • Freelancing opportunities
  • Client communication basics
  • Portfolio understanding

Module 12: Live Projects & Practical Training

Apply your knowledge through practical tasks and mini-projects to build real confidence.

  • Social media analysis
  • SEO practice basics
  • Content planning
  • Campaign strategy basics
  • Mini projects & final guidance
